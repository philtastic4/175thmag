Medals / Ribbons / Awards


Order of Precedence

Medal/Ribbon/Award
Description
175th MAG Achievement Ribbon
Awarded to those who have went above and beyond within the Group, has proven to be a valuable asset and is deserving of recognition. Recognition that does not qualify for a higher ranking award. This award can be submitted for recommendation by any of the squadron commanders.
175th MAG One Year Service Ribbon
Awarded to those who have actively in the 175th for one year.
175th MAG Six Month Service Ribbon
Awarded to those who have actively in the 175th for at least six months.
Operation Zero Tolerance
Awarded to those who have flown at least one mission throughout the Campaign (22DEC2021-31MARCH2022).
Syria Service Ribbon
Awarded to those who have actively served in the Syria Campaign (01).
175th MAG Contributor Ribbon
Awarded to those who have taken time to go above and beyond to contribute to the Group to help make the 175th MAG better. Which demonstrates great commitment to the group and makes the wearer a valuable asset to the team.
Training Exemplary Ribbon
Awarded to those who show excellence in training with outstanding teamwork, communication, risk and solid representation of the units SOP's, while going above and beyond to make themselves better and their unit better.
36th FS Cougars Service Ribbon
Awarded to those who have been or are currently attached to the Cougars as a squadron member.
VFA-22 Bengals Service Ribbon
Awarded to those who have actively served or are currently serving with the VFA-22 Bengals.
13th FS Panthers Service Ribbon
Awarded to those who have actively served or are currently serving with the Panthers.
A Co. AHC Jaguars Service Ribbon
Awarded to those who have actively served or are currently serving with the Jaguars.
175th Milsim Attack Group Service Ribbon
Awarded to those who have actively served or are currently serving with the 175th MAG.
