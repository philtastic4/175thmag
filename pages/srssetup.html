175th MAG SRS Setup

Downloading and Installing SRS:

1) Download and install SRS here by clicking on "DOWNLOAD LATEST RELEASE" here:

2) Scroll all the way to the bottom and click on and download "SRS-AutoUpdater.exe"
This will download the .exe file, run this once downloaded.

3) Once downloaded, open and install SRS. Get with your Squadron Commander immediately to setup and verify your installment.
This MUST be squared away prior to your first training night.
